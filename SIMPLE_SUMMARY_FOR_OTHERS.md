## 🎯 What is GrowStreams?

**GrowStreams is a payment streaming application on Canton Network that lets money flow continuously (every second) instead of in monthly batches.**

**Example**: Instead of paying someone $3,000 at the end of the month, GrowStreams pays them $0.001 every second as they work.

### 2. All Features Working ✅
**What you can do**:
- ✅ Create a payment stream (Alice pays Bob)

- ✅ Resume a stream (Alice resumes payment)

- ✅ Add more money to a stream (TopUp)
